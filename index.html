Chapter 01 — Foundation
Brand Foundation
品牌基礎
Owner: GPS Strategy & DX · Approver: GPS Director

Core Belief核心信念

"When people feel genuinely safe — not just at work, but across their whole lives — they are free to focus, create, and contribute at their highest level."

This belief is GPS's commitment — the reason this strategy exists. Everything we communicate, every tool we propose, every change we introduce must trace back to it. It is not a slogan. It is the standard against which every decision is measured.

這份信念是 GPS 的承諾——也是這項策略存在的理由。我們所溝通的一切、提出的每一個工具、引入的每一項變革，都必須回溯到這個核心。這不是口號，而是衡量每一個決策的標準。

Narrative Architecture 敘事架構

To honour that commitment, all change streams — AI adoption, brand transformation, operational evolution — must be narrated through a single coherent arc, not run as separate campaigns. A unified narrative reduces cognitive load, builds cumulative meaning, and gives every initiative a clear place in a larger story.

為了實踐這份承諾，所有變革脈絡——AI 導入、品牌轉型、營運優化——都必須透過一個統一的敘事弧線來講述，而非各自為營。統一的敘事降低認知負擔、累積意義，讓每個倡議都在更大的故事中找到明確的位置。

We define two phases as our near-term direction. Beyond that, the strategy does not end — it iterates. Stakeholder feedback gathered at the close of each phase shapes the next: its priorities, its narrative emphasis, and its channel mix.

我們定義兩個階段作為近期方向。此後，策略不會終結——它會持續迭代。每個階段結束時收集的利害關係人回饋，將形塑下一階段的優先順序、敘事重點和通路組合。

Brand Purpose品牌目的

To transform physical security from a compliance function into a human-centered enabling force — making safety invisible, intuitive, and deeply personal across every site where our people work.

將實體安全從一個合規功能，轉型為以人為本的賦能力量——讓安全在每一個工作場域中成為無形、直覺且深度個人化的存在。

Brand Mission品牌使命

Protect the world's most advanced semiconductor operations and the people within them by designing security systems that are precise, adaptive, and grounded in care.

守護全球最先進的半導體營運體系及其中的每一個人，透過設計精準、靈活且根植於關懷的安全系統。

Brand Vision品牌願景

A world where security is not felt as restriction but experienced as freedom — where every person entering our sites carries the confidence that their safety has been designed in advance.

一個安全不被感受為限制，而是被體驗為自由的世界——每一個進入我們場域的人，都帶著「安全已被提前設計好」的信心。

Phased Narrative Arc 敘事弧線階段性敘事弧線

Phase 1 · 2026

"We're changing how safety feels"

Challenge the assumption that security means restriction. Introduce new tools and ideas as expressions of care and support — for staff, for families, for the whole person. Establish a shared language across all sites.

Phase 2 · 2027

"Safety becomes who we are"

Transition from awareness to identity. GPS values are internalized by managers and reflected in day-to-day decisions — not only in formal communications. Cross-site communities of practice emerge.

Beyond Phase 2 — Continuous Iteration

Each phase closes with a structured review of stakeholder feedback — from security managers, site operations, partner functions, and contracted personnel. What we learn shapes the next phase: its priorities, its narrative emphasis, and its channel mix. The strategy improves because the people it serves inform it.

Philosophical Foundation哲學基底

CPTED — Crime Prevention Through Environmental Design. GPS's approach is rooted in designing environments that inherently reduce risk rather than relying solely on enforcement. This philosophy informs our visual language: open sightlines, natural surveillance, and environments that feel welcoming because they are secure.

CPTED——環境設計犯罪預防。GPS 的方法植根於設計本身就能降低風險的環境，而非僅仰賴執法。這一哲學影響了我們的視覺語言：開放的視線、自然的監控，以及因安全而感覺親切的環境。

Chapter 02 — Foundation
Brand Behaviors
品牌行為準則
Owner: GPS Strategy & DX · Approver: GPS Director

Brand behaviors define how GPS people act — observable actions that any colleague or visitor should recognize within the first 10 minutes at a GPS-managed site.

品牌行為定義了 GPS 人如何行動——這些是可觀察的行為，任何同事或訪客應該在進入 GPS 管理場域的最初 10 分鐘內就能識別出來。

Behavior 01
Explain Before Enforce先說明，後執行
Every security procedure has a reason. We communicate the "why" before enforcing the "what."每一項安全程序都有其理由。我們在執行「做什麼」之前，先溝通「為什麼」。
Behavior 02
Greet First, Verify Second先問候，再驗證
Human acknowledgment precedes security protocol. A greeting before badge verification signals we protect people, not perimeters.先以人的方式致意，再進行安全協議。在查驗門禁前先問候，表明我們保護的是人，不是圍牆。
Behavior 03
Design for the Uncomfortable Moment為不適時刻而設計
Security encounters are asymmetric. We proactively minimize discomfort, wait time, and ambiguity.安全互動本質上是不對等的。我們主動最小化不適感、等待時間和模糊感。
Behavior 04
Share What We Know分享所知
Safety knowledge is not proprietary. When GPS learns something that protects someone's family or commute — we share it.安全知識不是專屬情報。當 GPS 學到能保護某人家庭或通勤安全的知識——我們分享它。
Behavior 05 — Foundation
Treat Every Site as Someone's First Day視每個場域為某人的第一天
New hires, visitors, and transferred employees experience GPS before any other function. We are the first impression. Every interaction should meet the standard we would want for our own first day.新進人員、訪客和調派員工在接觸其他任何部門之前，最先體驗到的就是 GPS。我們是第一印象。每一次互動都應符合我們對自己第一天的期望標準。

Measurement衡量方式

Quarterly mystery visitor assessments, biannual safety perception survey (5 questions), and front-line supervisor peer reviews during shift handovers. Results feed into Brand Performance Metrics (Section 24).

季度神秘訪客評估、半年度安全感知調查（5 題）、以及換班交接時的第一線主管同儕評鑑。結果回饋至品牌績效指標（第 24 章）。

Logo Misuse標誌誤用

The appearance of the logo must remain consistent. It should not be reinterpreted, modified, or embellished. No attempt should be made to alter the logo in any way.

標誌的外觀必須保持一致。不得重新詮釋、修改或美化。任何人不得以任何方式改動標誌。

Misuse 01
Do not resize the mark不可重新調整圖標尺寸
The mark and logotype must always scale together proportionally. Never enlarge or shrink the mark independently of the text.圖標和標誌文字必須始終等比縮放。永遠不要獨立於文字單獨放大或縮小圖標。
Misuse 02
Do not rotate the logo不可旋轉標誌
The logo must always appear in its standard upright orientation. No angular rotation is permitted.標誌必須始終以標準直立方向呈現。不允許任何角度旋轉。
Misuse 03
Do not change the color of the mark alone不可單獨更改圖標顏色
The mark and text must share the same color treatment. Do not recolor the globe independently.圖標和文字必須共用相同的色彩處理。不要單獨更改球體顏色。
Misuse 04
Do not outline the logo不可將標誌轉為外框
The logo uses solid fills. Do not convert to outline, add strokes, or apply border effects.標誌使用實心填充。不要轉為外框、添加筆畫或套用邊框效果。
Misuse 05
Do not add gradients to the logotype不可對標誌文字添加漸層
The gradient belongs to brand surfaces, not the logotype itself. Keep the text portion single-color.漸層屬於品牌表面，而非標誌文字本身。保持文字部分為單一顏色。
Misuse 06
Do not stretch or manipulate不可拉伸或變形
Never distort, warp, squeeze, or disproportionately scale the logo. Maintain exact aspect ratios at all times.永遠不要扭曲、變形、壓縮或不等比縮放標誌。任何時候都要維持精確的長寬比。

Logo Clarity標誌清晰度

When creating a composition, it is important to have enough contrast between background and logo to maximize legibility.

在製作構圖時，確保背景與標誌之間有足夠的對比度以最大化清晰度至關重要。

正確Use the standard color logo on clean, light backgrounds. Use the white (reversed) logo on solid dark backgrounds. Ensure the background provides sufficient contrast — light solids, subtle gradients, or clear photographic areas all work.在乾淨的淺色背景上使用標準彩色標誌。在深色背景上使用白色（反白）標誌。確保背景提供足夠的對比——淺色單色、柔和漸層或乾淨的攝影區域皆可。
禁止Do not place the standard logo on same-tone colored backgrounds where contrast is insufficient. Do not place the reversed logo on busy, detailed, or low-contrast photographic backgrounds where the mark becomes illegible.不要將標準標誌放在對比不足的同色調背景上。不要將反白標誌放在繁忙、細節過多或低對比度的攝影背景上，這會使標誌難以辨識。

Visual Consistency視覺一致性

Three pillars govern visual consistency across all GPS outputs:

三大支柱治理所有 GPS 產出的視覺一致性：

Typography
Brand Fonts Only僅使用品牌字體
Stick with the brand fonts. Use approved heading/subheading/body text sizes for hierarchy. Max 3 font sizes in one sector to maintain readability.堅持使用品牌字體。使用核定的標題/副標題/內文字級維持層級。每區段最多 3 種字級以維持可讀性。
Color
Brand Palette品牌色盤
Begin with neutral or brand-approved colors. Sticking to the brand palette makes it easier to keep things consistent without needing design expertise. You may use topic-specific color themes, but avoid harsh neon tones or visually jarring combinations.以中性或品牌核准色開始。遵循品牌色盤能更輕鬆地維持一致性，無需設計專業。可使用主題專屬配色，但避免刺眼的霓虹色或視覺不協調的組合。
Graphics
Pattern for Highlight圖形用於強調
Use graphic pattern for highlight, not filler. The checkerboard elements should accent and complement, never overwhelm the content.使用圖形做強調，而非填充。棋格元素應做點綴和輔助，永遠不要壓過內容。

Visual Do's & Don'ts 視覺規範

Text Hierarchy文字層級

正確Use approved heading/subheading/body text sizes. Follow spacing and alignment structure. Use max 3 different font sizes/weights in one sector to keep readability and hierarchy. Rearrange layout based on your need — the structure is flexible.使用核定的標題/副標題/內文字級。遵循間距和對齊結構。每區段最多使用 3 種不同字級/字重以維持可讀性和層級。可依需要調整排版——結構是靈活的。
禁止Do not mix fonts or make everything bold. Do not stretch or shrink text manually. Do not center-align everything by default — left-alignment is the standard for body text.不要混用字體或全部加粗。不要手動拉伸或縮小文字。不要預設置中對齊——左對齊是內文的標準。

Illustration Style插畫風格

正確Use minimal, clean, vector-style illustrations. Maintain consistent stroke, perspective, and color. Prefer neutral or brand-accented palettes. Introduce custom topic-specific color accents carefully.使用簡約、乾淨的向量風格插畫。維持一致的筆畫、透視和色彩。優先使用中性或品牌點綴色盤。謹慎引入自訂主題色彩。
禁止Do not use mismatched clipart or stock cartoon packs. Do not combine different illustration styles with photo or 3D styles randomly. Maintain a single visual language per piece.不要使用不搭配的剪貼圖或素材卡通包。不要隨意混搭不同插畫風格與照片或 3D 風格。每件作品維持單一的視覺語言。

Color Usage色彩使用

正確Start with neutral or brand base. Ensure contrast between background and type. You may introduce custom topic-specific color accents carefully as long as they harmonize with the brand palette.以中性色或品牌色為底。確保背景與文字之間的對比。可謹慎引入自訂主題色彩，只要與品牌色盤協調。
禁止Do not use harsh neon tones or visually jarring combinations. Do not apply random rainbows or unbalanced color blocks. Do not color logos or type arbitrarily without ensuring sufficient contrast.不要使用刺眼的霓虹色或視覺不協調的組合。不要使用隨意的彩虹色或不平衡的色塊。不要任意為標誌或文字上色而未確保充分的對比。
