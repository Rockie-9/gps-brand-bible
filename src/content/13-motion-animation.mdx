### <span className="en">Audio & Sound</span><span className="zh">音效與聲音</span>

<p className="en">GPS does not maintain a sonic brand identity. Digital interfaces use system-default notification sounds only. Video productions may use ambient tone (low-frequency, non-melodic) but no branded music or jingles. Emergency communications follow standard alert tones per local regulation (OSHA, J-ALERT, etc.).</p>
<p className="zh">GPS 不維護聲音品牌識別。數位介面僅使用系統預設通知音。影片製作可使用氛圍音（低頻、非旋律性），但不使用品牌音樂或片頭曲。緊急通訊遵循當地法規的標準警報音（OSHA、J-ALERT 等）。</p>
