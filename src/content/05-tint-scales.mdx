### <span className="en">Scale Usage Guide</span><span className="zh">色階使用指南</span>

<p><span className="en">The 50–950 range serves different purposes. Lighter tints (50–200) for backgrounds and surfaces. Mid-range (300–500) for interactive elements and accents. Dark shades (600–950) for text and high-contrast elements.</span><span className="zh">50–950 範圍服務不同用途。淡色（50–200）用於背景和表面。中間色（300–500）用於互動元素和點綴。深色（600–950）用於文字和高對比元素。</span></p>
