### <span className="en">Safety Feeling Index (SFI)</span><span className="zh">安全感受指數 (SFI)</span>

<p className="en">The SFI is a quarterly survey measuring perceived freedom to focus, create, and contribute across 800+ global employees. It is a named index — not a generic perception survey — anchored to GPS's core belief that genuine safety enables peak performance.</p>
<p className="zh">SFI 是一項季度調查，衡量 800 名以上全球員工所感受到的專注、創造和貢獻的自由度。這是一個具名指數——而非一般性的認知調查——錨定於 GPS 的核心信念：真正的安全感能釋放最高績效。</p>
