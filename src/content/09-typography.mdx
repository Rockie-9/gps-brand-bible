### <span className="en">Type on Image</span><span className="zh">影像上的文字</span>

<p className="en">Typography can be applied on top of imagery. In these instances, use black typography on lighter images, and white typography on darker images to ensure that there is enough contrast. Never use colored typography on imagery.</p>
