### <span className="en">Alt-Text Writing Guidelines</span><span className="zh">替代文字撰寫指南</span>

<p><span className="en">Every image, SVG, and visual element in GPS materials must have appropriate alternative text. The alt text should convey the same information a sighted user would get — not just describe the image, but explain its purpose in context.</span><span className="zh">GPS 素材中的每張圖片、SVG 和視覺元素都必須有適當的替代文字。替代文字應傳達與視覺使用者相同的資訊——不只是描述圖片，而是在上下文中解釋其目的。</span></p>
