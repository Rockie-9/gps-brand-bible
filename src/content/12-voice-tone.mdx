### <span className="en">Audience-Specific Writing Rules</span><span className="zh">依受眾調整的寫作規則</span>

  <div className="card" style={{borderLeft:'3px solid var(--turquoise)'}}>
    <div className="card-label"><b><span className="en">For Frontline Staff / All-Staff 員工層</span></b></div>
    <div className="card-note">
      <span className="en">Simple language. Every concept paired with a concrete work or life example. Instructions framed as "you can do this" steps. Maximum reading level: Grade 8 equivalent. Avoid management jargon. Step-by-step format lowers the barrier to action.</span>
      <span className="zh">語言淺顯易懂。每個概念配一個具體的工作或生活場景例子。指引以「你可以這樣做」句型呈現。閱讀程度上限：國中二年級。避免管理學術語。步驟化格式降低行動門檻。</span>
    </div>
  </div>
  <div className="card" style={{borderLeft:'3px solid var(--turquoise)'}}>
    <div className="card-label"><b><span className="en">For Section Heads / Directors 主管層</span></b></div>
    <div className="card-note">
      <span className="en">Inverted pyramid: conclusion first, then evidence. Cite methodology or external sources with institution name, report year, or original link. Every strategy recommendation maps to a measurable indicator or concrete action. No vague assertions.</span>
      <span className="zh">倒金字塔結構：結論先行，再提供依據。引用方法學或外部資料時，附上具體佐證來源（機構名稱、報告年份或原始連結）。每個策略建議對應可衡量的指標或具體行動。避免空泛論述。</span>
    </div>
