### <span className="en">Philosophical Foundation</span><span className="zh">哲學基底</span>

<p className="en"><strong>CPTED — Crime Prevention Through Environmental Design.</strong> GPS's approach is rooted in designing environments that inherently reduce risk rather than relying solely on enforcement. This philosophy informs our visual language: open sightlines, natural surveillance, and environments that feel welcoming because they are secure.</p><p className="zh"><strong>CPTED——環境設計犯罪預防。</strong>GPS 的方法植根於設計本身就能降低風險的環境，而非僅仰賴執法。這一哲學影響了我們的視覺語言：開放的視線、自然的監控，以及因安全而感覺親切的環境。</p>
