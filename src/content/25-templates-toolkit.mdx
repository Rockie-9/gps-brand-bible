### <span className="en">Visual Do's & Don'ts</span><span className="zh">視覺規範</span>

#### <span className="en">Text Hierarchy</span><span className="zh">文字層級</span>

<DoExample
  doContent={<><b>正確</b><span className='en'>Use approved heading/subheading/body text sizes. Follow spacing and alignment structure.
